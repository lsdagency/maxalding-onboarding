# VSL Script guidelines

Document 2, built with build/vsl_script.py. Apply funnel-stage thinking (a paid-traffic asset). Same typography and header as Document 1, no date, logo at top.

## Format
Horizontal 16:9. This is a landing-page video, not a social ad. Do not instruct vertical filming. Recording best practice adapted for landscape: camera on a tripod, frame wide enough for shoulders and above.

## Five-step formula, in order
1. What it is (about 0:00 to 0:10): one or two sentences. Programme name, who it is for, what it does. No warm-up, no "hi my name is".
2. Why they need it (about 0:10 to 0:35): the problem in the ICP's own daily language. Empathetic, not dramatic. The viewer should think "that is exactly me".
3. What they get (about 0:35 to 1:00): transformation and outcomes only, no features or inclusions. What life looks and feels like after. Do not state price.
4. Why to trust you (about 1:00 to 1:25): a brief, human coach intro, then one real client result. Specific and believable. Only documented proof, framed as one person's experience.
5. Next step (about 1:25 to 1:45): position the private consultation as genuinely valuable in its own right (premium framing, never a free call). Explain what happens on it and what they leave with. End warmly, one clear instruction, no pressure.

## Rules
Full natural sentences, no two-word lines or fragments. Transformation focus throughout. No price. No jargon. No Meta, ads, funnel or marketing terminology. Direction note in italics then the spoken line in grey quotes, with the same timestamp format as Document 1.
